How to access the database?

Use the OEP-Dataview if you want to browse data and get an overview on what kind of datasets are available on the OEP and how they are structured. The OEP features functionalities for different views on datasets, such as graphs and maps. Use the OEP-API if you want to use the data published on this platform for your own purpose. You may also add new datasets to the platform, create new tables and much more. See the Wiki to start a tutorial.

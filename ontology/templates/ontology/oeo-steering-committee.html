Open Energy Ontology Steering Committee (OEO-SC)


What is the Steering Committee?

The OEO Steering Committee (OEO-SC) was established to enhance the visibility, adoption, and development of the ontology in ongoing and future projects. The committee conducts regular (about every two months), brief conference calls and personal meetings at scheduled events to discuss planned and completed tasks. Continuous communication between ontology developers and the OEO-SC is maintained via GitHub.
